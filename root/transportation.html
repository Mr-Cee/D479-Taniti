Transportation

This page makes transportation easy to find (a key tester request).

Getting Here

  • By air: Taniti has a small airport for small jets and prop planes. Expansion is planned for larger jets.
  • By cruise ship: A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Getting Around

  • Public buses: Taniti City buses run daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Private buses: Serve the rest of the island.
  • Taxis: Available in Taniti City.
  • Rental cars: Available near the airport.
  • Bikes: Bikes and helmets can be rented. Helmets are required by law.
  • Walkability: Taniti City is fairly flat and walkable. Merriton Landing is also easy to explore on foot.

Airport Notes

Plan extra time during busy travel weeks. If you are staying near Taniti City or Merriton Landing, many areas are walkable once you arrive.
