Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Banks offer currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as the United States).

Alcohol Sales Hours / Drinking Age

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18 (and it is not strictly enforced).

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Safety

Violent crime is very rare. As tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Holidays

Taniti has many national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants may be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan ahead.

Medical Care

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.
